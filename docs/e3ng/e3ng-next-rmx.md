## Steps for New belting method:

#### General / Prerequisites:
* Assemble X Gantry
* Ensure you have a stopper on the gantry (so Y does **not** move at all. ie. rubber stoppers)
* Ensure you have printed a [belt locker](https://www.printables.com/model/479348-voron-ab-belt-installation-helper-clamp) (print 5 just in case they break)
* Put the Stopper on the Y Gantry so the Y Gantry can not move more than 5mm
* At any point if the belt is not on the respective bearings, you can try a shoe shining motion to try to lower (or increase) its height to the bearings. **If the belts are not positioned correctly with the bearings, you can cause damage to your belts!**

#### Lower Belt (Left Motor):
* Put the LOWER belt through the tensioner and to the X carriage.
* Lock the belt using the belt locker
* Remove all motors (I would recommend removing electronics and the middle DIN rail to make it easier in case of issues)
* Route the belt to the motor and through the lower belt path.
* Fully Route the lower belt path **without the motor installed**
* Use a small alan key (skinny) and move it through the side to where the belt is.
* Turn the alan key and pull the belt to create a loop where motor B gets installed
* Reinstall motor B
* Lock the Lower belt on the other side of the X carriage and unlock the left side

#### Upper Belt Belt (Right Motor):
* Route the top belt through the left side of the X gantry (not the motors) and lock the belts on the left side
* Route the belts through the left motor mount (It will take a while, good luck)
  * You do not need to worry about position here, just do the shoe shining motion later if they get messed up!
* Route the belt to the X gantry and lock it at the **exact** same distance as the lower belt
* Use a small alan key (skinny) and move it through the side to where the belt is.
* Turn the alan key and pull the belt to create a loop where motor A gets installed
  * You do not need to worry about position here, just do the shoe shining motion later if they get messed up!
* Reinstall motor A
* Unlock the belts
* Route the belts through the tensioner
* Route belts through Gantry

Congratulations, you routed the belts. Good luck on the rest of your build!
